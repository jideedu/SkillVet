alexa red bus privacy policy red bus is a voice enabled application that allows users to get live bus arrival times in london
what information do we collect
we do not collect any "personal information"
non-personal information includes information that cannot be used to personally identify you, such as anonymous usage data
your device address - we use the device address set in your amazon alexa companion app so that we can find the arrival times of buses for the nearest bus stop
this information is deleted after a session ends
in order for us to gain access to your device address you need to explicitly grant our alexa skill permission to access your device address in the amazon alexa companion app when you first launch the skill
anonymous usage data - we may store and analyse how users interact with the application
all usage data is anonymised

we will notify you of significant changes to our privacy policy by sending a notice to the primary email address specified in your account or by placing a prominent notice on our site
significant changes will go into effect 30 days following such notification
non-material changes or clarifications will take effect immediately
you should periodically check privacy policy page for updates
