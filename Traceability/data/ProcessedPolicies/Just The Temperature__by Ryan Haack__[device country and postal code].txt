just the temperature privacy policy
this document will describe the kind of information we collect and how it is used
what information we collect
we collect your postal code and iso 3166-1 alpha-2 country code in order to get information about your local weather
this information is only gathered if you have granted the skill permissions to use your location in the alexa app
we do not intentionally retain any of the information gathered about your location or local weather
we use the openweathermap
org to get your local weather
your postal code and country code are sent to them in the clear
please refer to openweathermap
org's privacy policy for more information about how they might use the information we send to them
