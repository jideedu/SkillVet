‍ personal information collected about you
‍ in general, you can visit and navigate the service without providing any personally identifiable information
personally identifiable information is information which specifically identifies a particular user
such information could include a name, address, or telephone number
some users of the service will elect not to provide personally identifiable information
those individuals will remain entirely anonymous to us and any data which is collected pertaining to their use of the service shall be anonymous information
the only personally identifiable information which we will collect is that which you provide on the service
we may collect and store any personal information you enter on the servicewomen to us in some other manner, including by way of comment or other submission you upload or post to the service

‍ use of personal information
‍ any personal information which is collected and used on the service is done in order to provide you with the product(s)or service(s) which you have requested
when you submit personally identifiable information to us, you understand that you are agreeing to allow us to access, store,and use that information for those purposes
the purpose for which we collect any information will be identified before or at the time that it is collected
in most instances, the purpose for which personal information is collected will be clear, and consent may be implied, such as when your name, address, and payment information is provided
it should be noted that providing us with your personal information is entirely your choice
however, your choice to not provide certain information on the service may limit our ability to provide you with our product(s)and/or service(s)
you will not be required to consent to the collection, disclosure, or use of personal information as a condition to the supply of our product(s)or service(s), except as required to be able to supply said product(s) or service(s)
the personal information collected on the service will be limited to those details which are necessary for the purposes that are identified by us
with your consent, we may collect personal information from you in person, via telephone, or by corresponding with you via mail, facsimile, or the internet
any personal information may only be used or disclosed for the purpose for which it was collected unless you have other wise consented, or when it is required or permitted by law
moreover, personal information will only be retained for the period of time required to fulfill the purpose for which it was collected or as may be required by law
protection and disclosure of personal information
‍ all reasonable precautions will be taken to safeguard your personal information from any loss, unauthorized use, access, or disclosure
only authorized individuals will be permitted to have access to the personally identifiable information which is provided and collected on the service, and they may do so only for permitted business functions
we are committed to periodically reviewing and enhancing our security and privacy policies
please note that we will not sell or disclose any personally identifiable information which is provided on the service to any third parties, unless doing so is required to fulfill the purpose for which the information was provided
we want you to feel confident using the service
however, we do not guarantee that any unauthorized, inadvertent disclosures will never occur
we may share your personal information with our authorized service providers that perform certain services on our behalf
these service providers may have access to personal information needed to perform their functions, but are not permitted to share or use such information for any other purposes
moreover, we may be required by law enforcement or judicial authorities to provide personally identifiable information to the appropriate governmental authorities
upon the receipt of the appropriate documentation, such information requested by law enforcement o judicial authorities will be disclosed
we may also release information to law enforcement agencies or other third parties if we determine that disclosure is necessary to protect the safety and welfare of our personnel or to enforce our terms of use
furthermore, we may disclose personal information when we believe it is appropriate in connection with efforts to investigate, prevent, report, or take other action regarding illegal activity, suspected fraud, or other wrongdoing
‍ the service may use cookies
cookies are small data files which assign your electronic device a unique identifier when you use the service
through the use of cookies, we also may automatically collect information regarding your online activities, such as the pages you visit, the links you select, and the searches you conduct on the service
this information permits us to administer the service more efficiently and to provide a more tailored and user-friendly experience to you
you may set your browser to notify you when you receive a cookie or to prevent cookies from being sent
however, doing so may limit the functionality that we can provide you when you the service
moreover, any third parties which may be linked on the service may use cookies or collect other information when you go to their site
please note that we do not control the collection or use of your information by these entities
you should contact these entities directly should you have any concerns regarding their collection or use of information about you
transfer of personal information
‍ please note that customer lists and information are considered assets of our business
as such, should we merge with another entity or sell our assets to another entity, our customer lists and the information collected on the service, including personally identifiable information, would be included among the assets that are transferred
option regarding collection and use of personal information
‍ as a user of the service, you may opt to not provide us with certain personally identifiable information
however, this choice may result in you being unable to use certain features of the service because such information may be required in order for you to register as a user, purchase product(s)or service(s),or initiate other transactions on the service
‍ this policy will be periodically modified to reflect any changes in our practices and services
when such modifications are made, we will revise the “updated”date at the top of this policy
