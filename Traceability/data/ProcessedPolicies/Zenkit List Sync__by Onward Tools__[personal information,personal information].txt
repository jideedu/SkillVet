alexa terms of use

if you use this skill you fully agree to this privacy policy

we do not collect or share personal data with this skill
to improve our services we may need to manually review data collected, but will only do so with your explicit permission

your continued use of our skill after changes of the privacy policy or the skill itself will be seen as your acceptance of both
this skill stores current list items in temporary storage for the purposes of syncing between skills
items that are deleted from zenkit or alexa are synced (deleted from the other account) and then deleted from the data store
the entire datastore is deleted when the customer diables the skill
all data is hosted in aws and is not exported
the data collected is only used for the direct functionatliy of the skill
manual access is only for the purposes of debugging
