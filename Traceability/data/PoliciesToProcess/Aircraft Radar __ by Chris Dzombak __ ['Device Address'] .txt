aircraft radar skill for amazon alexa
aircraft radar uses your device’s address to find your location and search for aircraft
around you in response to your queries
your location information appears temporarily in a secured server log file which is
used for troubleshooting when necessary
that log is cleared out regularly
your information is never stored permanently and is not used for any purpose other than
the aircraft radar skill is developed and run by chris dzombak
