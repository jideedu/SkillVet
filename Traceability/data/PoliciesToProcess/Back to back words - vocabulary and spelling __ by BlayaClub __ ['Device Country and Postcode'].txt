personal information we collect
when you use the back to back words (named as schlangenwort in the germany version) alexa skill, we automatically collect your country code and your zip code
when we talk about “personal information” in this privacy policy, we are talking
about only your country and zip codes
how do we use your personal information
we are developing a research study to determine whether there is a relation between the area of residence and the words used
this study will bring totally anonymous results, that is why we only need your country and zips codes
how can you revoke your consent
disabling the permission in the skill settings screen
exactly at the same place where you granted the permission
