april 17, 2018 we take your privacy seriously picniic ("picniic," "we," "us," and "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of all its users
we understand and value the importance of keeping your family’s experience with picniic safe and secure
if you have any additional privacy or security questions after reading this information, please contact us
this privacy policy governs how we collect and use your information when you use our websites, mobile apps, and related services ("services")
how we collect and use information we collect and use the following types of information
information you give us
when you create an account, we may collect your personal information such as your username, first and last name, birth date, email address, mobile phone number and a photo, if you chose to have a photo associated with your account
if you want to add a child or family member’s name to your account, we collect the child or family member’s name, email address, and mobile phone number
we may collect billing information when you sign up for a subscription
in addition to personal information we may also collect data such as calendar appointments, shopping list items, and recipes
if you choose to send text messages to your family via picniic, we will collect phone numbers from you for that purpose
we collect information from and about your use of the services and the devices you use to access the services
this includes things like ip addresses, the type of browser and device you use, the web page you visited before coming to our sites, and information about the advertisements you view and the links you click on
your devices (depending on your settings) may also transmit location information and/or an advertising identifier to the services
if you upgrade to picniic premium from an ios or android app, you will pay for the upgrade directly through apple or google, respectively, and we will not have access to your credit card information
picniic’s use of your information we use the information we collect to provide, personalize, improve, offer, and protect our services
if you contact picniic, we may use your information to provide customer support to you
we may use your email address to communicate with you, for example, to inform you when a subscription is ending
picniic is able to provide many of our services free of charge because we are supported by advertising
in order to make these services available, we may use the information we collect to improve the advertisements you see by making them more relevant
does picniic share my information
we understand your family's information is important to you, and we treat it very carefully
we do not sell your personal information, and we do not share your personal information with third parties, except as described below
with vendors working for picniic
picniic uses certain trusted third parties to help us provide, personalize, improve, offer and protect our services ("vendors")
vendors will access your information only to perform tasks on our behalf, in compliance with this privacy policy
we may disclose your information to third parties if we determine that such disclosure is necessary to (a) comply with the law or in response to a law enforcement agency's request
(b) protect any person from death or serious bodily injury
(c) prevent fraud or abuse of picniic or our users
or (d) protect picniic's property rights
use of cookies cookies and other technologies
we use technologies like cookies and pixel tags to provide, improve, protect and promote our services
for example, cookies help us with things like remembering your account for your next visit, understanding how you are interacting with our services, and improving them based on that information
you can set your browser to not accept cookies, but this may limit your ability to use the services
protecting children's information picniic takes protection of your family's personal information very seriously
picniic will never knowingly request personally identifiable information or private content from anyone under the age of 13
in order to register and create a picniic account you must be 18 years of age or older
accessing or correcting information as a picniic user, you have the opportunity to review and modify your personally identifiable information and your private content any time you log onto your picniic account using an email address and password attached to the account
what if our business is sold
we reserve the right to transfer any information we have about you in the event of a merger with a third party or upon a sale or transfer of all or a portion of our business or assets to a third party acquirer
the new company would be required to protect all the information that picniic has collected from its users under the same terms of this privacy policy
how picniic protects your information picniic cares about the security of your information and uses commercially reasonable physical, administrative, and technological safeguards to preserve the integrity and security of all information we collect and that we share with our service providers
however, no security system is impenetrable and we cannot guarantee the security of our systems 100%
in the event that any information under our control is compromised as a result of a breach of security, we will take reasonable steps to investigate the situation and where appropriate, notify those individuals whose information may have been compromised and take other steps, in accordance with any applicable laws and regulations
what happens if i want to forward my picniic information to a friend
if you use one of the picniic features designed to allow you to voluntarily forward or share your information or content with family or friends outside of your secure account, we will forward or share access to such content from your account to your friend's email address as you provided it to us
such email address may be stored by picniic for your future use but will not be used by picniic to initiate any emails
by sharing your content, you automatically limit picniic's ability to keep your content private
we recommend that you do this only with trusted friends and family
