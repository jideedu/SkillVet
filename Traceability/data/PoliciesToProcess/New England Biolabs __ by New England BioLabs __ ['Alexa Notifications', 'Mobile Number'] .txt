we use cookies, web beacons, tracking pixels, and other technologies to collect information when you use and navigate the sites, as well as interact with emails from us

with cookies in place, our system will recognize you whenever you use your web browser to connect to our page
tracking pixels are transparent graphic images, sometimes called web beacons or tracking beacons, that are placed on a web page or in an email to indicate that the page or email has been viewed
pixels collect information about your browser or device and can also set cookies
we collect information when you use and navigate the sites or interact with our emails, such as the url of the site you came from, the browser software you use, your internet protocol (ip) address, ip ports, date/time of access, data transferred, pages visited, amount of time you spend on the sites, information about transactions conducted on the sites, and other “clickstream” data
