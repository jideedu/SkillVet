bin day west suffolk privacy and personal data
we do not collect or store any personal information via this web-app or the alexa skill
your postcode is passed straight to west suffolk council's website where it is used to look up bin collection data
we store your postcode in a cookie on your device
by using a cookie it means that we don't need to store any of your personal data on our servers
we use google analytics to help us monitor usage and flag errors
google analytics places a few cookies on your computer to track you throughout the web-app
