privacy policy and terms of use
at recycle coach, we collect information to provide better products, services, and experiences for all of our users – from figuring out your location in order to provide tailored recycling and disposal information, to device-specific information for sending custom notifications, to anonymized usage data for improving product usability
we only collect the data necessary to provide services to you and to improve our product
information we collect, how we collect it, and why
we collect several types of information from and about users of our website and apps, including information
by which you may be personally identified, such as your name, street address, e-mail address, social media handles, phone number and other identifying information that you choose to share with us securely (“personal information”)
that is about you but individually does not identify you, such as information about your internet connection, the equipment you use to access our website and apps and other usage details
we collect this information
directly from you when you provide it to us securely (e
when joining our mailing list or by signing up to receive collection reminders via in-app notifications, email, phone, sms, or twitter)
automatically as you navigate through our website and apps
information collected automatically may include usage details, ip addresses, and information collected through cookies, and is used to provide support, improve our products, and to display personalized content such a calendars and collection information
we collect some personal information from you (if/when you choose to share this information with us), such as your street address, e-mail address, location information in order to provide accurate and personalized content and services
information you provide to us will not be sold, rented, or leased to third parties
we also occasionally collect non-identifying information about you for analytics purposes, in order to improve your experience while using our web and mobile products
how we store and protect your information
we have implemented measures designed to secure your personal information from accidental loss and from unauthorized access, use, alteration, and disclosure
all information you provide to us through the website and app is secured by ssl technology and is stored on our secure, password protected servers behind firewalls, which are located in tier 4 hosting facilities in canada
we have implemented restricted employee access for all of our servers containing personally identifiable information, and conduct recurring staff training on relevant security practices
we take various steps to ensure the security of your personal information, including limiting employee’s access to sensitive data, encrypting and backing up the data, and using ssl technology for our website and mobile apps
we use industry-leading security and hosting standards and protocols to ensure the safety and integrity of your data
