your privacy and covid-19
in an effort to keep your equinox community safe in light of the covid-19 pandemic, we are collecting additional information from our members, employees, guests and other club visitors
our transparency is key to maintaining your trust, and during these unprecedented times, we are especially mindful of striking the right balance between ensuring the health and safety of our members, employees and the public and respecting your privacy
accordingly, effective as of july 16, 2020, the following privacy notice (the “privacy notice” ) shall apply in addition to our existing privacy policy , set forth below, which will continue to otherwise govern our collection, use and other processing of your information
as the current circumstances and the needs of our community evolve, this privacy notice may be modified or updated in equinox’s sole discretion
if we make any changes to our privacy notice, we will post the revised document here and such changes will be effective immediately upon that posting
please review this privacy notice periodically to ensure you are aware of any changes
what information we’re collecting
we may collect certain information about your potential exposure to covid-19 and health through periodic health declarations or questionnaires we ask you to complete
we may also collect measurements of your body temperature when you are at a club
we will not record or store your body temperature measurement, but if your body temperature registers higher than our permitted threshold for club access,
we reserve the right to record and indicate in our system that you were denied entrance and are not eligible to return for 3 days (or other time period specified in your health declaration)
in addition we may collect information provided by you, or a public health authority or other government agency, if you test positive for covid-19, including information about how you interacted with equinox during the time in which you may have been infected with covid-19
all of the foregoing is referred to in this privacy notice as “screening information”
what we’re doing with screening information
we collect and use screening information to help provide a safe environment for our employees and members, for public interest in protecting public health and to administer your membership and access to our clubs
we will use screening information in furtherance of those legitimate interests and business purposes
we will not use your screening information for any marketing or promotional purposes
how we may share screening information
while we generally will not share your screening information without your consent, we reserve the right to disclose this information, and other related information such as contact information, to government and regulatory authorities, such as the centers for disease control and prevention, and other health authorities in your jurisdiction, or as otherwise necessary or appropriate in furtherance of public health or safety
if you are a member or guest at one of our clubs in the united kingdom, please see below for additional information regarding nhs test and trace
if we are notified that you tested positive for covid-19 within 14 days after your last visit to equinox, or if we otherwise determine that you may pose a risk to our community, we reserve the right to disclose to our employees and members that an individual who tested positive, or otherwise poses a risk to our community, was in our facilities and while we will not disclose your name unless legally required to do so, we may disclose information such as the date and time of your visit, your gender, and club areas and amenities you used
we share this information to help ensure our clubs remain safe for our employees and other members
we may also share your screening information and other related information with service providers, advisors or consultants who are performing services on our behalf and for other reasons in accordance with applicable law, as further described in the “information sharing” section of our privacy policy below
please note we may disclose aggregated, de-identified information for any other lawful purpose

if you are a member of, or other guest or other visitor at, one of our clubs in the united kingdom, please note , we may share information that we already collect about you, pursuant to our privacy policy below, through your club booking and check-in, specifically, your name, contact phone number, date of club visit, arrival time and estimated departure time ("visit information"), with nhs test and trace for purposes of supporting their contact tracing service and complying with their requests

your screening information will be protected as described in our privacy policy below, and will be retained only as long as we determine reasonably necessary to ensure the health and safety for our members, employees, guests and other club visitors and in accordance with applicable law
