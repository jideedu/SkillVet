digital orlando collects aggregated information regarding user traffic patterns mainly by tallying page views throughout the sites and mobile apps (“usage tracking information”)

a cookie cannot read data off your hard disk or read cookie files created by other sites
digital orlando uses cookies to track user traffic patterns (as described above)
