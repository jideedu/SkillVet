revised june 29, 2017 – the ohio lottery recognizes the importance of privacy to visitors of the ohio lottery web site

usage statistics in order to generate usage statistics to help us improve our web site, we automatically collect certain information on every visitor to the web site
however, this information does not disclose “personal identifiers” (such as name, address, telephone number, e-mail address, etc

examples of this type of information includes the type of internet browser or computer operating system you are using, the date and time you accessed this site, the internet protocol address of the individual computer (or proxy server) making the request, and the internet address of the site from which you may have linked to this site
