collection of your personal information

sharing information with third parties

brewer media group may share data with trusted partners to help perform statistical analysis, send you email or postal mail, provide customer support, or arrange for deliveries
all such third parties are prohibited from using your personal information except to provide these services to brewer media group, and they are required to maintain the confidentiality of your information
brewer media group may disclose your personal information, without notice, if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to
(a) conform to the edicts of the law or comply with legal process served on brewer media group or the site
(b) protect and defend the rights or property of brewer media group
and/or (c) act under exigent circumstances to protect the personal safety of users of brewer media group, or the public

this information can include
your ip address, browser type, domain names, access times and referring website addresses
this information is used for the operation of the service, to maintain quality of the service, and to provide general statistics regarding use of the brewer media group website
this website contains links to other sites
please be aware that we are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of such other sites
we encourage our users to be aware when they leave our site and to read the privacy statements of any other site that collects personally identifiable information
brewer media group does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under the age of thirteen
